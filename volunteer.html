Volunteer with us

Our programs are designed with a concept of volunteering at our centre which will enable boost our labour force enable you and our people to interact and exchange ideas ,learn local languages ,culture and in exchange share happy moments with our children at our centre, give hugs to them as a sign of being cared and loved for, encourage them to work hard in their academics as these doesn't mean they can't make it life despite their childhood misfortune ,through these one will leave a lasting memory. These program provides an opportunity for volunteers to experience the host country not normally experienced with an ordinary tourist.

We do encourage volunteers to donate learning materials ,drugs ,clothing and food as most essential of all and if any feels touched with the livelihoods of the children in our centre ,then he or she can donate money as well, as we do entirely depend on such kind of generous donations for our daily running of the institution since all Community Based Organization hardly get funding from Kenyan Government.

Getting to kenya

Kenya is well served with the world's airlines which fly either to join international Airports Nairobi or Moi International Airport -Mombasa.


Leaving the airport

Airport Cars (Taxes) charge US$ 50 or its equivalent rate is payable when leaving the airport to residential areas, apartments and to the hotels within the city (Nairobi)


Programme fees

The programme fee depends on the duration of your visit,please email us your specific plan ,qualification and the mission.
Our programmes run from two weeks to months but we can arrange special arrangements if you can stay longer. Also you can have a look at the FAQ's ?
2 weeks   US$   550
1 Month   US$    850
2 Months  US$  1450
3 Months  US$  1750

The programme fee cater for

  • Accommodation : We do organization accommodation in Modern Apartments and in an average cost hotels which are safe and pleasing to stay in comfortably.
  • Meals - Usually breakfast and dinner, meals taken outside the project compound like hotels one has to pay from his or her pocket.
  • Air Transfers  Fees for student passes/permits  (government regulations) please visit (www.immigration.co.ke)
